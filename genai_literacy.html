Welcome — this is not a normal quiz

Most quizzes ask you to pick the right answer. This one asks you to defend it.

For each real-world scenario you'll choose the best response and write why. Then you'll see an expert's reasoning, self-check your justification against a rubric, and get probing follow-up questions to push your thinking further. The difficulty adapts to how you do.

How it works

  1. Read a scenario you might actually face.
  2. Choose the strongest response and justify it in writing.
  3. Reveal the expert reasoning and honestly self-assess your justification.
  4. Reflect on the Socratic follow-ups — no right answer, just better thinking.

Your progress is saved on this device only. Nothing you write is sent anywhere — it stays in your browser.
