Welcome, Conservationist!

How to Play

Click on an animal's icon to generate Conservation Points. Use those points to level up animal programs, hire managers for automation, and buy upgrades to boost production.

Your Goal

Restore each species' population to safe levels and remove them from the Endangered Species List. Click any animal's icon to learn more about it!

Tips

  • Higher-level programs produce more points per cycle
  • Managers automate production so you earn while idle
  • Upgrades multiply your output significantly
  • Reach population milestones to rescue species!
